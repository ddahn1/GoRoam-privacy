GoRoam Support

Questions or need help? Email us at support@approamly.com.

We typically respond within 2–3 business days.

Report a Problem

  1. Describe what you were doing and what went wrong.
  2. Include screenshots if possible and your app version (Settings → About).
  3. Mention device model and OS version.

For privacy requests (access/delete data), include “Privacy Request” in the subject line or see our Privacy Policy.
